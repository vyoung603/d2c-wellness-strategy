D2C Wellness Strategy

Expanding SonderMind's digital mental health home from therapy support to broader wellness platform

Internal / Project Juno / June 2026

02 / 07

Why This, Why Now

  • SonderMind serves ~15M therapy-seekers/year. The digital wellness market (Calm, Headspace, Flo, Liven) has proven 100M+ downloads exist. That's a 6-7x expansion of who we can serve.
  • We're already paying to reach these people. Tens of thousands bounce off sondermind.com monthly -- not ready for therapy, no insurance match, not sure. Today they leave. The app gives them a home.
  • CMS ACCESS launches July 5 and requires a non-therapy digital experience. We're building it regardless. D2C is the same experience with a different entry point.
  • Some wellness users become therapy referrals -- a new lead gen channel. But the model works independently of therapy conversion.

03 / 07

The Shift

Today: Therapy App with Wellness Features

Therapy is the core. Wellness content exists alongside it. Non-therapy users get a partial experience. Marketing drives to sondermind.com for therapist match.

Tomorrow: Wellness App with Therapy Module

Wellness is the core. Therapy exists as a module -- available when needed, never assumed. Like Tesla's app adding solar alongside your car. The app works perfectly without it.

  • The app becomes wellness-first. Personalized pathways, daily activities, AI coaching. Therapy is a module you add when you're ready.
  • We build this differently -- AI personalizes pathways at scale, adapts the experience in real-time, accelerates creative production. We ship in weeks, not quarters.
  • The experience must be delightful, frictionless, and consumer-grade. The bar is the best apps people use daily, not healthcare portals.
04 / 07

The Marketing Realignment

The consumer acquisition strategy shifts all the way upstream. Demand capture continues, but a substantial portion of brand awareness and upper-funnel demand gen spend can drive to the app rather than pushing everyone into therapy.

Today

Upper-funnel spend drives to sondermind.com. Non-converters bounce and leave entirely. We spend more reducing friction for people who may never be ready for therapy.

Tomorrow

Brand campaigns drive to a frictionless app download. One channel, pathway-specific creative, direct to app store. Non-converters from the website get routed to the app -- not abandoned.

  • Keep friction in the therapy match flow intentionally. Focus the zero-friction experience on app onboarding.
  • The app becomes the home for everyone not ready for therapy today. When they are ready -- we identify it and send them to therapy.
  • This touches performance, lifecycle, brand, and product marketing. All need to understand the shift and be prepared to play their part.
05 / 07

What the App Becomes

A personalized, engaging consumer experience connected end-to-end -- from the ad that brought you in through daily use.

  • Marketing creative connects to onboarding connects to daily experience. No disconnect, no generic landing.
  • Assessment-driven personalization from second one. Your first activity, first Sonder conversation, first notification -- all informed by why you're here.
  • AI adapts the experience over time. Sonder remembers yesterday. Pathways evolve based on your progress. The app gets better the longer you use it.
  • Beautiful, consumer-grade UX that competes with the best apps people actually open every day.
  • Existing therapy users aren't disrupted -- they get an app update, their experience lives in the Care module, and the wellness layer enriches rather than replaces what they already have.
Total Brain at the Core
Clinical-grade assessments that drive personalization. 100+ evidence-based activities that form the pathway content. Measurable outcomes that prove it works. This is what Lontra validated and what no competitor has. Calm gives you a library. Headspace gives you a course. Liven has pathways but no intelligence behind them. We have the clinical engine to make personalization real.

Appendix

Supporting Detail

Early thinking on execution, experience design, and retention

What We Built with Lontra

The standalone D2C path wasn't right, but the assets are validated and ready to power the SM app's wellness expansion.

  • Built a complete consumer wellness experience in weeks
  • TB assessments modularized into consumer-friendly Brain Fitness Scores
  • 100+ evidence-based activities connected to personalized pathways
  • Sonder AI coach validated in standalone wellness mode
  • Asset stack (TB + activities + Sonder + SM infra) is unique vs generic wellness apps

Building Differently with AI

Different approach to how we build, what tools we build with, and what tools we build for our members.

How We Build

Define the clinical framework and evidence base. AI personalizes sequencing, pacing, and content for each user. Ship in weeks, not quarters. Content and pathways scale without requiring individual sprint cycles for each one.

Tools We Build With

AI generates pathways at scale. Accelerates creative production and testing. Adapts the daily experience based on user behavior. The app improves without new deploys.

Tools We Build for Members

Sonder as a personal coach with context. Personalized care pathways that adapt to progress. Assessments that drive real personalization. An experience that gets better over time.
